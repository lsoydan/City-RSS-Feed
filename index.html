This site is a prototype currently being tested by the Chattanooga Innovation Team (i-team) — it was built in direct response to what residents told us they need: better access to updates, information, and resources. The Chattanooga i-team works alongside community members, local organizations, and city departments to turn gaps into opportunities. We are still working to improve this tool based on resident feedback, please take our survey to let us know what would make this tool more useful.
